Plan your party at Brats!!

Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or just going out for the night, we want to host your group! Submit this form with your group's information and we will get back to you with special deals just for your group (you will need to provide a guest list).  Any size parties welcome.

Upon arrival, you and your guests will get wristbands and access to your personal specials.  Your party may be private - have your own section of the bar) (which will require a small room fee) or open in which case you will not have your own part of the bar but will still have your personal specials.

We look forward to hearing from you!
